# Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at Zhejiang University, under the guidance of [Prof. Shouling Ji](https://nesa.zju.edu.cn/webpage/crew/jsl.html). I received my B.S. degree with honors in Computer Science and Technology from Ningbo University (Yangming Class), advised by Prof. [Diqun Yan](http://www.yandiqun.com/).

My research mainly focuses on Trustworthiness of Generative AI and Distributed Learning Systems. I have been working on several projects related to trustworthiness, especially advsarial and backdoor attack&defense, and privacy-preserving deep learning.
